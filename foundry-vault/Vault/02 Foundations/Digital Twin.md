# Digital Twin

> The Ontology as a living model that mirrors the real organization and updates as it changes.

The [[Ontology & Metadata#Ontology|Ontology]] is meant to be a faithful, current model of the business's entities, relationships, and decisions. As operations change, the model changes; security and state stay live rather than static. Its forward-looking, simulatable dimension — [[Scenarios#Scenario|scenarios]], [[Models, Inference & Simulation#Model|models]], and [[Models, Inference & Simulation#Simulation|Simulation]] over a temporal substrate — is the [[Ontology — Dynamic Layer|dynamic layer]].
