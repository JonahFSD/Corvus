# The Closed Loop

> Foundry's defining motion: data flows up into meaning, decisions flow back down into systems of record.

Raw data is integrated, given meaning as objects, acted on through governed [[Actions#Action Type|actions]], and the decision is written back to systems of record. The platform is part of operations, not a window onto them. Every other capability exists to make some part of this loop faster, safer, or smarter.
